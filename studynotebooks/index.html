Closing Concept Knowledge Gaps

Before You Begin

When you first missed this question, did you feel like you had to guess because you forgot the underlying concept?

Yes → Continue with this protocol. A knowledge gap is likely present and this resource is the right next step.
No → Stop here. Bring the question and your error log to your next session. This is most likely a process or perception error, not a content gap, and a different intervention applies.

Using Your Notebook

Left Panel

Sources. The vetted videos and documents loaded into this notebook. You do not need to open these. They are what NotebookLM draws from when responding to you.

Center

Chat. Your main workspace. Type any question or prompt here and NotebookLM will answer using only the sources inside this notebook. You will need a free Gmail account to interact.

Right Panel

Studio. Pre-built tools you can generate on demand: Audio Overview, Flashcards, Quiz, Mind Map, and more. Use these after your chat session to reinforce or test what you reviewed.

Audio Overview

A conversational summary of all sources. Good for a quick listen before diving into the chat.

Flashcards

Auto-generated cards from the source material. Use these to drill key definitions and rules.

Quiz

Run this before you leave. If you can answer confidently, the concept has transferred.

Mind Map

A visual layout of how concepts connect. Useful if you are working across a broader topic area.
